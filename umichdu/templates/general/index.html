Delta Upsilon, an international fraternity founded in 1834 at Williams College on the principle of non-secrecy, is committed to building better men by espousing the four founding principles: the promotion of friendship, the development of character, the diffusion of liberal culture and the advancement of justice.

Founded in 1876, the University of Michigan chapter has been a stable on campus for well over a hundred years and continues to contribute to every aspect of campus life: academic, athletic, social, student government and philanthropic. Our iconic 1903 chapter house was completely rebuilt and finished in August 2012, allowing DU to continue building on its venerable tradition at Michigan.

We welcome you to our website and invite you to explore the past, present, and future of the University of Michigan Chapter of Delta Upsilon.
